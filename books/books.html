My Online Library

There’s a pattern in these types of people, at least one element in their formula for success: they’ve all consumed hella books for fun and curiosity, and still continue to do so.

So I decided to incorporate this habit.

I now understand the power of a book. You can just feel and swim in someone’s thought stream. Incredible!
