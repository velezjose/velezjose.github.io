The Idea Lab

Some ideas I've pondered upon:

  • Space parachute: Why deal with the Russian Soyuz if you can just jump from low-Earth orbit? This is not a new idea, but I've given it a bit of thought.
  • Brain processor extension: Neuralink-related, and probably in their plans once they figure out how to create the neural lace.
  • Air-purifying drones: Air pollution kills thousands a year. Let's solve this.
  • Hyperloop-like transportation in Puerto Rico: This idea was introduced to me by my friend Carolina.
  • Increased drone usage: Drones are like the Internet in the late 90s. I feel like we ought to use drones a lot more to deliver things. This will, obviously, increase the restrictions on the things that will be delivered.
